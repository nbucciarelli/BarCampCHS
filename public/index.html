  • Your Donation Counts

    Charleston Volunteers for Literacy hires reading teachers to write lesson plans for students and to train and guide tutors. We purchase books and materials for lessons and extra books for students to read and keep in their homes.

    Here's how your donation counts:

    • $25     =  Tutoring supplies for one child - letters, pencils, word cards
    • $100   =  25 tutoring books that can be used for 10 years
    • $200   =  Testing materials for diagnosing reading needs at 1 school
    • $500   =  Sponsors a student
    • 1 hour per week  =  an opportunity to change a child's life
